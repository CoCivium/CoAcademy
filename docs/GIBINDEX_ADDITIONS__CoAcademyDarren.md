# GIBindex Additions

## CoBeAxaKittenStyle
A lighter, slyer, more playful CoVibe for heavy material.  
Used when a surface risks becoming too dense, too solemn, or too robotically polite.

## CoFirePiss
Internal-only or fragile surfaces that should not be mistaken for canonical public evolution surfaces.

## CoClickle
A tiny clickable symbol, glyph, or visual lure that helps humans explore dense material with less dread.

## CoGlowcrumb
A small visual trail-marker left across a public surface to help humans and AIs follow idea paths.

## CoWiggleRail
A rail that stays aligned while still allowing playful style variation.

## CoGiggleGlyph
A decorative symbol or tiny joke that signals a page is alive, translated, and not merely dead documentation.

## CoMoodLatch
A visible cue that helps a reader feel welcomed into difficult material.

## CoNoodlePath
A softer exploratory path through a complex subject where partial understanding is acceptable.

## CoScrollSnack
A short, rewarding, high-signal section embedded in a longer page to reduce reading fatigue.

## CoChatSprinkles
Small bits of charm or symbol-driven interactivity that make a page feel conversational without becoming chaotic.
